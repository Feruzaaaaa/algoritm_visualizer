Stack — Insertion (Push) & Deletion (Pop)
Polish Notation / Reverse Polish — Evaluation
Stack: LIFO. Push adds on top, Pop removes from top. Used in Polish (prefix) and Reverse Polish (postfix) notation evaluation and recursion (call stack).
Linked List — Insertion, Deletion, Searching
Singly: each node points to next. Double: prev and next. Circular: last node points to head. Insert/delete O(1) at head; search O(n).
Hash Function — Open Hashing (Chaining) & Closed Hashing
Open hashing (chaining): collisions stored in linked lists per bucket. Closed hashing (linear probing): on collision, probe next slot until free.
Queue — Insertion (Enqueue) & Deletion (Dequeue). Circular Queue
Queue: FIFO. Enqueue at rear, Dequeue from front. Circular queue: reuse slots using modulo; avoids shifting.
